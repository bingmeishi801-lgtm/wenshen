description

1. Tattoo Idea

A memorial-inspired tattoo concept representing growth, memory, and personal resilience.

2. Meaning

This piece is meant to honor a meaningful chapter in life and serve as a reminder of healing, strength, and continuity. The client wants the tattoo to feel personal and elegant rather than overly literal.

3. Visual Elements

Olive branch Soft sun Fine line leaves Minimal initials Light organic flow

4. Style Direction

Fine Line

Delicate, minimal, and refined with clean detail and a subtle overall feel.

5. Placement

Inner forearm

Chosen for visibility, personal meaning, and a shape that works well with a vertical composition.

6. Size Guidance

Medium

Large enough to preserve detail, but still subtle and easy to wear.

7. Notes for Artist

The client would like this piece to feel elegant, personal, and not too busy. They are open to the artist refining the final composition and balancing the placement with the body line.
