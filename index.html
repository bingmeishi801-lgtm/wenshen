Frequently asked questions

Is Before You Ink a tattoo generator?

Before You Ink is not just a tattoo generator. It is a consultation prep tool for first-time tattoo clients. It helps you clarify your idea, compare style and placement options, and create a brief your artist can actually use.

Do I need tattoo knowledge to use it?

No. Before You Ink is built for people who may not know tattoo styles, sizing, or placement terms. The guided flow helps you think through the important decisions in a simple way.

Will this replace my tattoo artist?

No. Your tattoo artist should still create, refine, or adapt the final design. Before You Ink helps you communicate more clearly before the consultation.
